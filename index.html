A Trip to Florida

A mesmerizing trip which made memories....

Day 1

On the first day, we visited Disney land. My children were excited about meeting mickey mouse, see the princesses and visit the other disney characters.

Day 2

On Second day, we went to Sea World to see the aquatic life and all the marine animals. Also, we took all the spectacular roller coaster rides and 3-D rides. The Shamu show, dolphin show and Sea Lion & Sea otter show were breath-taking. It makes me feel that animals are no less than human-beings in intelligence.

Day 3

Third day was dedicated to space, so we visited NASA. We were thrilled about visiting Nasa and seeing all the rockets & space shuttles. We got picture clicked with an astronaut. It was a life-time experience.

Day 4

Day four, we went to Universal Studios. A major attraction that I always wanted to visit in my life. So, the excitement level was at its peak. We took pass for Universal Studios. I make sure that I don't miss any ride and repent afterwards.
